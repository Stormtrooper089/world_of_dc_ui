Privacy Policy

SMC Karmachari — Silchar Municipal Corporation Attendance App

Effective date: 1 June 2026 Last updated: 1 June 2026 App: SMC Karmachari (com.smc.karmachari)

1. Overview

SMC Karmachari is an internal attendance and work-tracking application for field workers employed by the Silchar Municipal Corporation (SMC), Cachar District, Assam. The app is not available to the general public — access requires an account created by an authorised SMC supervisor.

This policy explains what personal data is collected when you use the app, how it is used, who can access it, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data Collected

DataWhen collectedPurpose
Mobile number Account registration (by supervisor) Login identifier — OTP authentication
Name & address Account registration Identifying the worker in attendance records
GPS location Attendance mark-in and mark-out Verifying attendance at work site
Camera photos Attendance check-in and work uploads Visual proof of presence and work completed
Attendance timestamps Each mark-in / mark-out action Daily attendance record generation
Device location (background) Not collected — foreground only N/A

The app does not collect contacts, call logs, SMS messages, browsing history, advertising identifiers, or any financial information.

3. How Data is Used

  • Generating daily attendance reports for SMC supervisors
  • Verifying field worker presence at assigned work sites
  • Maintaining official municipal employment records as required by government regulations
  • Enabling supervisors to monitor squad-level attendance via the DC Office web portal

Data is not used for advertising, sold to third parties, or shared outside the Cachar District Office and SMC administration.

4. Who Can Access Your Data

RoleAccess level
You (the worker) Your own attendance history and uploaded photos
Squad supervisor Attendance and photos of workers in their assigned squad
SMC officers / DC office All attendance records across all squads
Third parties None — data is not shared externally

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encrypted).
  • Data is stored on a MongoDB Atlas database hosted within secure cloud infrastructure.
  • Your login session token is stored in the Android Keystore (hardware-backed encrypted storage) on your device — not in plaintext.
  • Photos are stored on secure cloud servers and are not publicly accessible.
  • No sensitive data is stored in unencrypted device storage.

6. Data Retention

Attendance records and work photos are retained for a minimum of 3 years in accordance with government employment record-keeping requirements. Account data is retained for as long as the employment relationship is active.

Upon termination of employment, account access is revoked by the supervisor. You may request deletion of your data by contacting the SMC office (see §8).

7. Permissions Used by the App

PermissionWhy it's needed
Fine & Coarse Location Recording GPS coordinates at attendance mark-in and mark-out
Camera Taking mandatory selfie photo at attendance check-in and for work-progress uploads
Internet Syncing attendance data and photos with the SMC server

All permissions are requested at the time of first use and can be denied via device settings.

8. Your Rights

As a registered user of this app, you have the right to:

  • Access your attendance records and uploaded photos
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise any of these rights, contact your squad supervisor or the SMC office directly.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
